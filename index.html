Places to Cry on Campus!

The Back of the Campus Connector

There is no greater feeling than getting on a Campus Connector in the winter, just after sunset. The bus is mostly empty, the lights are on and the bus is just warm enough that you can slip off your pair of gloves to shuffle a Spotify playlist and stare dreamily out the window. It is in these moments of peace that members of the student body can feel safe... safe enough to cry.
